Chapter 1: Introduction to Physics

Physics is a vast and diverse field that encompasses a wide range of topics, from the smallest subatomic particles to the entire cosmos. It is a field that is constantly evolving, with new discoveries and advancements being made every day.
In this chapter, we will introduce the basic concepts and principles of physics, including the scientific method, the laws of motion, energy, and thermodynamics. We will also explore the history of physics and the contributions of some of the most influential physicists in history.

The third law states that for every action, there is an equal and opposite reaction.
